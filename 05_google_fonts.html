GOOGLE_FONTS

Google Fonts (formerly known as Google Web Fonts) is a computer font and web font service owned by Google. This includes free open source font families, an interactive web directory for browsing the library, and API's for using the fonts via CSS and Android. Google fonts is also used with the Google workspace..

The fonts in Google Fonts are hosted on Google's servers, making them easily accessible for anyone with an internet connection. They are also optimized for web use, ensuring fast loading times and consistent rendering across different devices and browsers.

One of the key features of Google Fonts is its ease of use. Designers can browse the collection, select the fonts they want to use, and integrate them into their websites using simple CSS code provided by Google. Additionally, Google Fonts offers a variety of customization options, allowing designers to adjust font weights, styles, and character sets to suit their needs.

Google Fonts is widely used in web design due to its accessibility, variety of fonts, and ease of integration. It has helped democratize typography on the web by providing high-quality fonts for free to developers and designers worldwide.
